Why do we need roles?

When developing a product it is important for workers or students to organize their workspace in a way that best suits their long term goals. A well planned out workspace will improve the efficiency of both product development and presentation. In addition, it is important to establish an appropriate division of work as this will help in the final integration process.

What is a scrum?

A scrum is a term in the sport rugby (shown in fig 2), which is a close-knit group of people in an offensive huddle. Similarly, a scrum master manages smaller groups inside of a larger overall group (fig 3). A project team can have many scrum teams and scrum masters, with one overall project manager. Scrum masters are generally focused on the project requirements, and helps each subunit in the overall team to stay on track and meet their individual goals. Scrum teams generally work in short periods of time to achieve realistic goals, these time periods are known as sprints.
